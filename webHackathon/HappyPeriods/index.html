About Us

Happy Periods is an initiative to create Period Ready Schools and villages in India.

We conduct sessions on menstruation in schools and communities with girls of age 12-15, their mothers, teachers and Asha workers(Local health worker of the village).

Video learning material 'Hello Periods' from Menstrupedia.org, few body positive art activities and charts of female reproductive systems are used in the session.

Moving forward towards creation of period ready schools and villages, pink dustbin for disposal of sanitary waste are also to be set up in schools and communities and a booklet of five activites which has to be done by the girls themselves after talking to their mothers and peers to ensure that they are period ready.
